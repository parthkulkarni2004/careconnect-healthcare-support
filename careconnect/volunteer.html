Frequently Asked Questions

Can I register as a volunteer without prior experience?

Yes! We welcome passionate individuals from all backgrounds. While prior experience is helpful, what matters most is your willingness to learn and help others.

Is volunteering time-bound?

No, you decide your own schedule. We offer flexibility with weekday, weekend, or flexible availability options.

What training will I receive?

We provide comprehensive orientation and training specific to your volunteer role before you start assisting patients.
