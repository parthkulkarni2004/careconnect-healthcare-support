Popular Questions

How can I volunteer?

Visit our Volunteer page and fill out the registration form with your details and area of expertise.

What support services are available?

We provide medical assistance, blood donation support, counseling, medicine support, and emergency assistance.

Is registration free?

Yes! All registration and services on CareConnect are completely free for both patients and volunteers.

How do I request assistance?

Go to the "Request Support" page, fill out the form with your details and healthcare needs, and submit. Our team will review and connect you with a volunteer.
